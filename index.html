Here are some of my projects

  • MangaTosho || E-Commerce Project

    Html5, CSS, Javascript, React

    React E-commerce app that displays data of recommended and popular shoujo (a specific genre) manga. MangaTosho gives users the ability to not only sort through manga via Price (High to low, low to high) & Rating but also gives them the opportunity to add their favorites into a cart for purchase.

  • Defend Your Home || Text Adventure Project

    Html5, CSS, Vanilla Javascript

    Defend Your Home is a text adventure game, where you can play as 4 different mythological creatures that are defending their manor from a horde of humans! Eight endings are possible. Will you be able to defend your home? Or perish when the humans ultimately surround and overwhelm you?

  • Ashley's All-Out Dog Training

    Html5, CSS

    Ashley's All-Out Dog Training helps connect dog owner's in the Austin, TX area to local certified trainer, Ashley Schmalz. The webpage is completely responsive on all devices for accessibility.

  • FureverFriends Project

    Html5, CSS, Vanilla Javascript, REST API's

    Using Petfinder's REST API, FureverFriends uses their database to relay local pets found in the Austin Pets Alive shelter that are ready for their new home! Furever Friends also allows users to use the search bar to find the best pet according to their needs.
